Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 — Dasrat WiFi Speed Test

Overview

Dasrat is a client-side speed test application. It runs entirely in your browser and does not send any personal data to Dasrat servers because there are no Dasrat servers. All measurement requests go directly from your browser to Cloudflare's public speed test CDN.

Data We Do Not Collect

Dasrat does not collect, store, or transmit your IP address, device information, browser fingerprint, test results, location data, or any personally identifiable information. No analytics platform is loaded. No cookies are set by this application.

Cloudflare Speed Test Endpoints

Speed measurements are made by sending requests to speed.cloudflare.com, a publicly available CDN endpoint operated by Cloudflare, Inc. Cloudflare may log these requests in accordance with their own privacy policy, which can be reviewed at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Dasrat has no control over and no access to any data that Cloudflare may collect from these requests.

Local Storage

Two preferences are saved in your browser's localStorage to improve your experience across visits:

dasrat_theme — your chosen interface theme (light or dark).
dasrat_unit — your chosen speed unit (Mbps, MB/s, or GB/s).

These values never leave your device. They are not transmitted anywhere and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this page.

Third-Party Scripts

This application loads one external resource: the DM Mono and DM Sans typefaces from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may log font requests in accordance with their privacy policy. No other third-party scripts, tracking pixels, or analytics tools are loaded by this application.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the change. We will not introduce data collection without clearly communicating it here.
